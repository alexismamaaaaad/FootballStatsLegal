# Privacy Policy - foot_stats_mediterranee

**Last Updated: January 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how "foot_stats_mediterranee" (the "App") handles data when you use the TikTok API.

### 1. Data Collection
The App only accesses data that you explicitly authorize via the TikTok OAuth screen. This may include:
* Basic profile information (username, avatar).
* Public video statistics (views, likes, shares) for football content analysis.

### 2. Data Usage
The data is processed locally on your device to generate football statistics and visualizations. We **do not** store your personal data on any external servers.

### 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. All data remains within the scope of your personal use of the App.

### 4. Data Retention
User data is kept only for the duration of the active session. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok security settings.

### 5. Contact
For any questions, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
